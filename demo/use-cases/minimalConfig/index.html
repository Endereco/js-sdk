MiniConfig

This test focuses on using a minimal configuration with only the mandatory settings.

ApiKey

To use our services, a personal ApiKey is required. This can easily be requested at https://endereco.de. There you will also find all further information about our services.

Person Service

Using the Person Service, personal data such as salutation, title, first name, and last name can be validated.

Address Service

Our Address Service is particularly comprehensive and enables the verification of addresses. Our complex examination takes into account the interplay of all necessary components of a valid address and focuses on returning an actually existing and deliverable address.

Phone Service

The Phone Service is used to verify phone numbers, including a valid country code.

Email Service

With the help of the Email Services, email addresses can be checked not only for a valid format but also for their actual existence and reachability.
